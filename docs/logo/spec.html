PWA launcher icon.
Renders on the home screen at the OS-imposed corner radius. The icon's own 22% radius is close to Android's adaptive-icon mask, so it nests cleanly.

A maskable variant is also exported (maskable-512.png) — same artwork without baked corners, full bleed, for Android adaptive icons. Reference it in your manifest with "purpose": "maskable".
